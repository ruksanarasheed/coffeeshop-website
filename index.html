Welcome to Rifan Coffee Shop, where passion meets plate. Established 2016, we've been dedicated to creating an exceptional dining experience for our cherished guests.

At Rifan Coffee Shop, we believe that a good cup of coffee or tea can be a gateway to a world of flavors and experiences. Our skilled baristas meticulously select and brew our beans and leaves to perfection, ensuring each sip is a delightful revelation.

Why Choose Us?

Our coffee shop isn't just a place to grab a quick pick-me-up. It's a space to relax, unwind, and connect.
